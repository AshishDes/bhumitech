Our story

Named for the ground you build on

Bhumi means earth — the ground beneath everything. We chose it because the best technology is exactly that: a dependable foundation you stop thinking about because it simply works.

Since 2011 we've grown from a small New York consultancy into a global delivery organization, while keeping the thing that started it all — senior people who care about your outcome, not just the deliverable.
